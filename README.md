# The History of Nanotechnology — Seminar Presentation

Presented by **Ms. Amruta Gautam Salve** — M.Sc. (Chemistry), Research Scholar, Maulana Azad College of Arts, Science & Commerce, Aurangabad.

Under the guidance of **Dr. Pramila P. Ghumare** and co-guidance of **Dr. Samreen Fatema**.

## About

A zero-dependency, animation-rich HTML presentation covering the history, synthesis, and chemical–physical applications of nanotechnology through to nanomedicine. Optimized for large-hall projection.
